Privacy Policy

Kenya Pro's League — Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

Kenya Pro's League ("KPL") is a private football tournament management platform operated by the League Administrator. The platform is accessible at okuku743.github.io/kenya-pros-league.

2. Data We Collect

When you register as a league member, we collect the following information:

  • Game Username — publicly visible to all league members
  • Real Name — visible to the League Administrator only
  • Phone Number — visible to the League Administrator only
  • Email Address — visible to the League Administrator only

We also collect chat messages posted in the Group Chat, which are visible to all league members.

This platform uses Google Firebase for data storage and Google Analytics for usage tracking. These services may collect technical data such as device type, browser, and IP address in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Why We Collect Your Data

Your data is collected solely for the following purposes:

  • To register and identify you as a league member
  • To enable communication between league members and the Administrator
  • To manage tournament fixtures, results, and standings
  • To improve platform performance through anonymised analytics

4. Who Can Access Your Data

  • Game Username: visible to all league members
  • Real Name, Phone, Email: visible to the League Administrator only, protected by a secure PIN
  • Chat messages: visible to all league members
  • We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal data to any third party

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore), protected by server-side security rules that restrict unauthorised access. The platform is served over HTTPS to ensure encrypted data transmission.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no system is completely secure. You participate at your own risk.

6. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data from the platform
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact the League Administrator directly through the platform chat or announcements.

7. Cookies

This platform uses browser local storage to remember your cookie consent preference and chat username. Google Analytics may use cookies to track anonymised usage data. By accepting the cookie banner, you consent to this use.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data requests, contact the League Administrator via the KPL platform announcements or Group Chat.
